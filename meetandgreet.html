My Meet & Greet with Young Thug

This picture was taken shortly after his performance at The Rave in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, hands down one of the best days of my life.

During the meet and greet, the security told me, "Don't think about getting a bro hug with him, because you won't." Surely, it was my turn to meet him and he liked my shirt, so he dapped me up anyway.
